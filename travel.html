My Favorite Countries

France

France is a beautiful country and I was so lucky to be able to visit it. There are many different places in France, Paris which is more like a city and it is very popular and has famous locations like the Eiffel Tower and Notre Dame. There is also a town called Nice, France which is more beachy and more rich than Paris.

Italy

Italy is also a very beautiful country. The food is exceptional especially the pizza. There are many hills in Italy and the views there are incredible. In Italy there are many different markets and they are well priced and the stuff is very unique.

England

England is super cool. There are so many different attractions in London, including the London Eye, which is very fun. The buses are very fun to ride and you will never forget the sound of the tube.
